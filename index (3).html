I am Bindia Kumari Thakur, a student of BSc Mathematics Honours (4th Semester) at Surya Sen Mahavidyalaya. My academic foundation in pure mathematics has cultivated an almost obsessive appreciation for structure, proof, and elegant reasoning.

This same mindset translates directly into how I approach web development — every layout, every function, every interaction deserves the same rigor you would apply to a mathematical proof.

I am passionate about logical problem-solving, clean interface design, and the intersection of analytical thinking with creative technology. When I am not studying theorems, I am building tools that reflect discipline and clarity.
